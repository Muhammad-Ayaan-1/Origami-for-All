LEARN HOW TO MAKE A TULIP

In Just 14 Easy Steps

Follow these steps respectievly
in order to make a perfect tulip!

Step: 01

Start with the white side up. Fold in half diagonally and unfold

Step: 02

Fold in half diagonally the other way and unfold. Turn the paper over

Step: 03

Fold in half vertically and unfold

Step: 04

Fold in half horizontally and unfold. Turn the paper over

Step: 05

Collapse the model into a Water-bomb Base

Step: 06

Fold the bottom corners of the top layer up to the center line

Step: 07

Turn the model over and repeat step 6

Step: 08

Flip one flap to the right in the front and back

Step: 09

Fold the flaps of the top layers slightly past the center line

Step: 10

Open up the left ‘pocket’, and insert the right flap inside

Step: 11

Turn the model over and repeat steps 9-10

Step: 12

Fold and unfold the bottom section from both sides

Step: 13

Blow into the bottom hole to inflate and flatten the model. The bottom should be a square shape

Step: 14

Peel out all 4 outer layers to create the petals

Final Result

Here is your origami tulip!
