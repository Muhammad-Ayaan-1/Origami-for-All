LEARN HOW TO MAKE A TURTLE

In Just 11 Easy Steps

Follow these steps respectievly
in order to make a perfect turtoise!

Step: 01

Start with the white side up. Fold in half vertically and unfold. Then, turn the paper over

Step: 02

Fold the edges to the center line. Then, turn the paper over

Step: 03

Fold to the center line, along one layer only. Make sure not to crease the flaps created in the previous step (see picture)

Step: 04

Fold up, aligning the bottom edge with the center line

Step: 05

Fold the right flap down, aligning the reference point with the bottom edge

Step: 06

Repeat on the left side

Step: 07

Fold the top flap in half

Step: 08

Fold the top flap up, aligning the circled points

Step: 09

Fold and unfold, aligning the side edges with the top edges

Step: 10

Swivel fold towards the center, using the crease made in the previous step

Step: 11

Pleat the bottom to create the tail. Then, turn the model over

Step: 12

Pleat the tail and narrow the body

Step: 13

Narrow the arms and legs

Final Result

Here is your origami turtle!
