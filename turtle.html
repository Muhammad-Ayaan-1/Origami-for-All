LEARN HOW TO MAKE A TURTLE

In Just 10 Easy Steps

Follow these steps respectievly
in order to make a perfect turtoise!

Step: 01

Start with the colored side of the paper facing up. Fold and unfold in half both horizontally and vertically. Then, turn the paper over

Step: 02

Fold and unfold both diagonals

Step: 03

Collapse the paper as shown on the diagram using the existing creases, making the waterbomb base

Step: 04

Make a small cut along the line shown in the diagram, cutting through both layers

Step: 05

At the back of the model, fold the left flap to the right

Step: 06

Bring the top right flap toward the center line and fold along the dotted line

Step: 07

Fold the top right flap over to the left side

Step: 08

Again, fold the top right flap over to the left side

Step: 09

Bring the top left flap toward the center line and fold along the dotted line

Step: 10

Fold the top left flap over to the right side

Step: 11

Fold the top corner down along the dotted line to create the head

Final Result

Here is your origami turtle!
