LEARN HOW TO MAKE A CUP

In Just 05 Easy Steps

Follow these steps respectievly
in order to make a perfect Cup!

Step: 01

Fold the paper in half

Step: 02

Fold the right corner of the paper over to the left edge along the dotted line

Step: 03

Fold the left corner over to the right edge just like you did in the previous step

Step: 04

Fold the top layer of paper down and tuck it into the little pocket

Step: 05

Fold the bottom layer of paper behind the model

Final Result

Here is your origami Cup!
